Our story

Your Trusted Local Auto Repair Shop in Dallas

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our community safe and mobile, building lasting relationships through transparency and expertise.

East Side Auto Repair was founded in 2008 with a simple mission: offer Dallas drivers a better choice for auto repair. Frustrated by dealership markups and chain shop upselling, our founder Michael opened a shop focused on honesty, quality, and customer education. What started as a two-bay garage in East Dallas quickly grew into a trusted neighborhood staple.

Today, we continue that tradition with a team of ASE-certified technicians and a commitment to transparent communication. We treat every vehicle as if it were our own, using advanced diagnostics and quality parts to ensure lasting repairs. Whether it's a simple oil change or a complex engine rebuild, you can count on East Side Auto Repair for fair pricing and exceptional service.
