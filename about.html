Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Auto Repair Shop

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our customers safe and satisfied. We believe in transparency, integrity, and exceptional workmanship.

East Side Auto Repair was founded in 2005 with a simple mission: offer reliable, honest auto repair to the Dallas community. What started as a small two-bay shop has grown into a full-service facility serving thousands of customers. Our team of ASE-certified technicians shares a passion for cars and a commitment to doing the job right the first time.

Over the years, we've earned a reputation for fair pricing and clear communication. We don't believe in upselling unnecessary repairs or cutting corners. Every vehicle that leaves our shop is backed by our warranty and our promise. We're proud to be a local business that treats every customer like a neighbor.
